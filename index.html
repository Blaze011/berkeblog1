How did I get here?

We have participated a competition that has been regulated by TTGV at Turkey, İstanbul. The competetion was making a project using Arduino about 3 titles. Titles were; physic, environment and education. We were a team with 11 members named A-RKOD from Arel College. We made a prototype arm at physic category and we qualified to finals. Judges analyzed our project and we have been elected to top 2 projects. Then they gave us this camp as a prize. Thats how I came here.
